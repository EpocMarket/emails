Setting up your athenaText App on your iPhone

Install the App
1. Open safari on your iPhone device and enter the following url:

http://tinyurl.com/luq7pk9

2. The link will take you to a download page. Please select "Download". When the popup screen appears,

Tap "Install".
3. The athenaText app will begin to download. When the download is complete, tap on the athenaText icon on your device home screen.

Login
4. When the app opens, you will be asked to Login or Create an Account.

Tap "Login", and use your athenaNet email and "Password1" for the password.

(ex:bnix@athenahealth.com).
5. To use the application, you will need to accept the terms of service, privacy policy, and business associates agreement. You can read them by clicking on the links next to the checkbox on this page.
Phone Settings
6. Once you have logged in, you will be asked to enter your mobile phone number to help us confirm your account and connect you with other colleagues. NOTE: We will not share your mobile number with anyone and we will not use it for advertising or marketing activities.

Please enter the phone number associated with your mobile device and tap "Continue".
Once you enter your mobile number, you will receive a text message containing a confirmation code.

Enter the code and tap "Confirm".
7. To be compliant with HIPAA regulations, you need to have a passcode set up for your device or for the application. Please select from one of the following options:

If you have a passcode on your device, you can select "Use my device passcode" and confirm that you have configured one. You can also set up a device passcode and acknowledge that you have done that.

If you would like to set a passcode for the application, please select "Mobile device passcode" and create a four-digit passcode. After you have created the passcode, please enter it a second time to confirm.

Please enter a code that you will remember.
Connect with the Community
8. Currently, we limit community access to athenaText to physicians (MDs and DOs). If you are a physician, in order to make it easier for you to find colleagues who may also be using athenaText, you will be asked if you would like to sync your phone contacts / address book.

If you answer "Yes", we will determine if any of your contacts are also athenaText users. If your phone number is also in their address book, we will add them to your personal "Colleagues".
Confirm Your Profile
9. Your profile is how others will see you in the application. Please confirm that your profile is set up correctly in the application.

If there are any errors, please contact your practice manager or athenahealth account representative.
Congratulations!! You can now start using the athenaText application.

Setting up your athenaText App on your Android Device

Important Note
1. Please change your Android settings before starting the installation.

You must allow installation of apps from unknown sources on your device.

Go to "Settings" -> "Security". Select "Unknown Sources".

This must be selected in order to install the application.
Download and Install the App
2. Open a web browser on your device and enter the following url:

http://tinyurl.com/nea3cvd
3. A pop-up will appear confirming that you would like to download the application.

Tap "OK".
4. The athenaText app will begin to download.

When the download is complete, go to your notifications screen and the row for the athenaText app.
5. You will be prompted to install the application. We will not access any information on your device without prompting you and getting your explicit permission.

Click "Next" to begin the installation.
6. When the installation is finished, you can tap "Open" to launch the application, or find the athenaText icon in your application tray or on the home screen.
