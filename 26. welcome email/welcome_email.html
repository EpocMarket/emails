Requirements
• Android phone with a firmware version of 1.6 and higher
  (Note: Android OS 3 is not supported).
• Minimum available memory: 2.1MB of main memory
  + 138MB* on the memory card.
• Wireless connection to download, sync and update.
 
Updates
To ensure that your clinical content is the most current available, including continually-updated drug and disease information, we recommend you update weekly:
 
1. Open Epocrates on your Android
2. Swipe to the left screen and tap "Updates"
3. Tap "Update Now"
 
Free Online Access
Access the most up-to-date content with Epocrates Online for FREE using your computer or laptop. It contains the same drug reference and drug interaction feature you currently have on your Android.
