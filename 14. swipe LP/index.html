Setting up your Epocrates Secure Text Messaging (STM) App on your iPhone

Install the App
1. Open safari on your iPhone device and enter the following url:

http://tinyurl.com/qck9bgk

2. The link will take you to a download page. Please select "Download". When the popup screen appears,

Tap "Install".
3. The STM app will begin to download. When the download is complete, tap on the STM icon on your device home screen.

4. When the app opens, you will be asked to Login or Create an Account.

If you already have an Epocrates account, tap "Login".

If you do NOT have an Epocrates account, tap "Create Account" and go to Step 6.
Login
5. Enter the email and password for your Epocrates account and

tap "Login".

If you have forgotten your password, tap "Forgot Password?". Proceed to Step 7.
Create an Account
6. To create an account, enter the information requested and

tap "Next". When you have completed the information on the screen and reviewed the Terms of Service,

tap "Join".
Verify Your Identity
7. We have a two part verification to ensure that you are sending and receiving messages to/from the right person:

Part 1: Physician verification
Part 2: Identity verification.

If you already completed our physician verification, proceed to Step 8. Otherwise, please enter the information requested and tap "Next".
8. Our identity verification service is provided by LexisNexis. You will be asked to enter your home address and optionally, the last four digits of your social security number. After providing this information, you will be asked 4 questions to confirm your identity. You must answer the questions correctly in order to proceed.
Phone Settings
9. Please enter the phone number associated with your mobile device and tap "Continue".

NOTE: We will not share your mobile number with anyone and we will not use it for advertising or marketing activities.
Once you enter your mobile number, you will receive a text message containing a confirmation code.

Enter the code and tap "Confirm".
10. For security purposes, please create a four-digit passcode. After you have created the passcode, please enter it a second time to confirm.

Enter a code that you will remember.
11. In order to make it easier for you to find colleagues who may also be using STM, you will be asked if you would like to sync your contacts. If you answer "Yes", we will automatically make them your "Colleagues".

Congratulations!! You can now start using the STM application.

Setting up your Epocrates Secure Text Messaging (STM) App on your Android Device

Important Note
1. Please change your Android settings before starting the installation.

You must allow installation of apps from unknown sources on your device.

Go to "Settings" -> "Security". Select "Unknown Sources".

This must be selected in order to install the application.
Install the App
2. Open a web browser on your device and enter the following url:

http://tinyurl.com/qck9bgk
3. A pop-up will appear confirming that you would like to download the application.

Tap "OK".
4. The STM app will begin to download.

When the download is complete, go to your notifications screen and the row for the STM app.
Login
5. You will be prompted to install the application. We will not access any information on your device without prompting you and getting your explicit permission.

Click "Next" to begin the installation.
6. When the installation is finished, you can tap "Open" to launch the application, or find the STM icon in your application tray or on the home screen.
7. When the app opens, you will be asked to Login or Create an Account.

If you already have an Epocrates account, tap "Login".

If you do NOT have an Epocrates account, tap "Create Account" and go to Step 9.
Login
8. Enter the email and password for your Epocrates account and tap "Login". If you have forgotten your password, tap "Forgot your password".

Proceed to Step 10.
Create an Account
9. To create an account, enter the information requested and tap "Next".

When you have completed the information on the screen and reviewed the Terms of Service, tap "Join".
Verify Your Identity
10. We have a two part verification to ensure that you are sending and receiving messages to/from the right person:

Part 1: Physician verification
Part 2: Identity verification

If you already completed our physician verification, proceed to Step 11.

Otherwise, please enter the information requested and tap "Next".
Your information is not stored by Epocrates and will not be shared with anyone

Enter it and tap "Next".
11. Our identity verification service is provided by LexisNexis. You will be asked to enter your home address and optionally, the last four digits of your social security number. After providing this information, you will be asked 4 questions to confirm your identity. You must answer the questions correctly in order to proceed.
12. Please enter the phone number associated with your mobile device and tap "Continue".

NOTE: We will not share your mobile number with anyone and we will not use it for advertising or marketing activities.

Once you enter your mobile number, you will receive a text message containing a confirmation code. Enter the code and tap "Confirm".
13. For security purposes, please create a four-digit passcode. After you have created the passcode, please enter it a second time to confirm.

Enter a code that you will remember.
14. In order to make it easier for you to find colleagues who may also be using STM, you will be asked if you would like to sync your contacts. If you answer "Yes", we will automatically make them your "Colleagues".

Congratulations!! You can now start using the STM application.
